## Disqualification Criteria

Move to "nurture" list (quarterly check-ins) if:

- Explicit "not interested" (respect their time)
- No response after 3 follow-ups over 60 days
- Forwarded to generic support email (wrong contact)
- Company announces competing verification initiative
- Acquisition/shutdown announced (M&A kills partnerships)

**Do NOT disqualify** for:
- Slow response (enterprise sales cycles are 3-12 months)
- Technical concerns (address with data)
- Pricing objections (offer pilot first)
