## Three-Track Monetization Plan

### Track 1: IMMEDIATE CASH — Consulting (Weeks 1-4)
- Market: $11B, growing 26%/year
- Rates: $300-500/hr (AI engineers at $900/hr per Fortune)
- Engagement types:
  - AI code quality audit: $20K-40K per week
  - LUCID integration pilot: $40K-80K per month
  - Advisory retainer: $5K-10K/month
  - Value-based: 10-40% of defect reduction savings

### Track 2: VIRAL ADOPTION — GitHub Action (Weeks 2-8)
- GitHub Action runs on every PR, produces verification badge
- Free for public repos, paid for private
- Pricing: $20/mo (Pro), $15/user/mo (Team), $49/user/mo (Enterprise)
- Viral mechanic: badge on PR = ad for LUCID
- Target: 1% of 150M GitHub users try it, 5% convert = 75K customers = $18M ARR

### Track 3: THE BIG PLAY — Training Signal Sales (Months 2-6)
- LUCID cost per preference pair: ~$0.25
- Human RLHF cost: ~$100 per comparison
- **400x cost advantage**
- Target customers: Mistral, Cohere, Together AI, Fireworks AI
- Pilot: 10K verified preference pairs for $50K (costs ~$2.5K to produce)
- Full deal: $500K-5M/year licensing

### Supporting Play: Build + Publish Model (Month 4-6)
- NOT for direct revenue — model weights don't sell
- Purpose: proof that training signal works at scale
- HuggingFace leaderboard placement = free marketing
- Case study for Track 3 sales conversations
- Cost: $1K-2K

1. **HuggingFace models don't make money directly.** Community consensus: "sell access, outcomes, or integration — not the model itself."

3. **Lovable went from 50K GitHub stars to $206M ARR in 12 months.** Open source -> commercial is the fastest proven path.
4. **Surge AI bootstrapped to $1.2B selling RLHF data.** Training signal sales are a proven business.
